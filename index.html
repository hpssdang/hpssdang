Why Hamro Pahunch Secondary School?

  • Our school is offering a full range of academic and pastoral opportunities for students to have a very individually created educational program for each student. HPSS allows for student growth in faith and strong values of acceptance, honesty and compassion and this drives our student welfare philosophy
  • Our school offers skillful and qualified teachers with excellence certification in their relevant subjects. The courses are taught by practicing professional, the instructors bring real world and real time expertise directly into the classroom.
  • Students are encouraged to learn subjects matter in an intuitive way and focused in practical approach rather theoretically. We provide well managed practical classes, class rooms and well facilitated laboratories for better understanding where students can demonstrate or visualize their knowledge ability towards their relevant study program.
